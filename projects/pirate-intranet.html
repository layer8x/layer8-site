Pirate Intranet

A secure over-the-air transparent meshnet

Codename Pirate Intranet is a project to build a reliable, scalable, and secure private meshnet for running distributed intranet applications. We are still in the planning phases of development but this is what we have so far.

We have chosen Ubiquiti to provide the necessary networking equipment.
We aren't sure which antenas will be used, but we plan on purchasing a few NanoBeams for initial testing.

Antena choices

NanoBeam AC

High-Performance airMAX® ac Bridge

The Nanobeam AC is probably the most sensible option as it is the most supported. It provides a good balance of range and speed for the price.
Pros:

  • Well supported
  • Very fast (450+ Mbps)
Cons:
  • Limited frequencies (only 5 GHz)

PowerBeam AC

High-Performance airMAX® ac Bridge

The PowerBeam is the second choice. It's basically a more powerful NanoBeam.
Pros:

  • Long Range (30+ km)
  • Very fast (450+ Mbps)
Cons:
  • Limited frequencies (only 5 GHz)

NanoBridge M

airMAX® Bridge

The NanoBridge is the third choice. It's basically a NanoStation with a dish.
Pros:

  • Long Range (30+ km)
  • Wide Range of frequencies (900 MHz, 2.4 GHz, 3/3.65 GHz, 5 GHz)
Cons:
  • Limited frequencies

NanoStation M

Indoor/Outdoor airMAX® CPE

The NanoStation is very well supported like the NanoBeam. Small and easy to install.
Pros:

  • Wide Range of frequencies (900 MHz, 2.4 GHz, 3/3.65 GHz, 5 GHz)
  • Well supported
Cons:
  • Not as fast as other options
  • (Possibly) suseptible to interference

Rocket M

airMAX® BaseStation

Super high performance base station, very highly customizable
Pros:

  • Wide Range of frequencies (900 MHz, 2.4 GHz, 3/3.65 GHz, 5 GHz)
  • Depending on frequency, can be very fast
Cons:
  • Expensive
  • Requires external antena (more $$$)

PowerBridge M

airMAX® Bridge

Light footprint like the NanoStation with more range
Pros:

  • Wide Range of frequencies (3/3.65 GHz, 5 GHz)
  • Longer range (20+ km)
Cons:
  • Not well supported
  • Not as fast as other options.
